WELCOME TO JUSTUS'S KITCHEN

👨‍🍳 TODAY WE WILL BE COOKING CHICKEN FRIED RICE 👨‍🍳

FEEL THE TASTE!!!

INGREDIENTS:

  • Cooked rice - 2 to 4 cups
  • Chicken breast - 150 g
  • Eggs – 2 to 3, lightly beaten
  • Garlic – 2 to 3 cloves, minced
  • Onion – 1 large, chopped
  • Carrots – 1, diced
  • Peas – 1 to 1.5 cups
  • Bell peppers – ½ cup, chopped
  • Soy sauce – 2 to 4 tablespoon
  • Sesame oil – 1 to 2 teaspoons
  • Vegetable or canola oil – for frying
  • Salt and pepper – to taste

PREPARATION STEPS:

  1. Cook 2–3 cups of rice and let it cool completely. Cold rice works best to avoid mushiness.
  2. Dice 150g of chicken breast or thigh.
  3. Marinate with a pinch of salt, pepper, and a bit of egg white or cornstarch for tenderness.
  4. Heat 1½ tablespoons of oil in a wok or pan.
  5. Stir-fry the chicken for 3–4 minutes until nearly cooked through.
  6. Move it to one side of the pan.
  7. Whisk 1–2 eggs and pour into the empty side of the pan.
  8. Scramble until just cooked, then mix with the chicken.
  9. Remove both from the pan and set aside.
  10. Wipe the pan and add ½ tablespoon oil.
  11. Add 1 tablespoon minced garlic and fry for 30 seconds.
  12. Toss in chopped spring onion whites, bell peppers, and optional chilies.
  13. Stir-fry on high heat for 2 minutes until veggies are crisp.
  14. Add the cooled rice to the pan.
  15. Sprinkle black pepper and pour in a sauce made of:
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
    • ¼ teaspoon sugar
  16. Mix and fry on high flame for 1–2 minutes.
  17. Stir in the cooked chicken and scrambled egg.
  18. Fry until everything is heated through.
  19. Taste and adjust seasoning with more soy sauce or salt if needed.
  20. Garnish with chopped spring onion greens.
  21. Serve hot and enjoy!
